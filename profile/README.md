# Fish Slapped Mayhem

Fish Slapped Mayhem is a working group of one, maintaining a portfolio of approximately twelve concurrent
initiatives across infrastructure, media orchestration, applied machine learning, and exploratory tooling.
The exact figure varies. Project status is reviewed when convenient.

The organization was established to provide a coherent home for work that has, on close inspection, more
in common than its surface variety would suggest. The evidence for this is not yet entirely available. The
investigator is confident it will surface in due course.

## Areas of Activity

The current portfolio includes, but is by no means limited to:

- Infrastructure and tooling, broadly construed
- Applied machine learning and adjacent scaffolding
- Media pipeline experimentation
- Signal processing of various descriptions
- Whatever has most recently caught the attention of the responsible party

A more complete index is maintained elsewhere, and is approximately current.

## Contributions

Contributions are not presently accepted, on the grounds that no procedure for accepting them has been 
established. Inquiries may be directed through the usual channels, which are likewise unestablished. The matter 
is under review.

## Resources

Documentation, where it exists, lives in the repository to which it pertains. Documentation, where it does not 
exist, is on the roadmap. The roadmap is itself in the process of being documented.

## Fun Facts

The organization observes no fixed schedule. The organization maintains no formal mascot, though one has been 
informally proposed and is awaiting committee review. The organization's preferred breakfast is black coffee and 
the cautious optimism that today's deploys will hold.
